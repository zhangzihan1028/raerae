About Me?

    The family environment made it impossible for me to break free from the embrace of art. My father was a musician and formed his own band, and my brother is an excellent young director who started a high-quality live music video program that is well-known in China. Under the influence of such a family environment, I, who was born in the Qianxi year, naturally became interested in Design in an era when art and trends were developing so rapidly. I often fantasize that I have a studio of my own, work with like-minded partners to create our own different art, lead new trends and cultures, and "open doors" for China.
